About Us

The Centre for Composite Materials, established within the prestigious CEG Campus of Anna University, is dedicated to serving the diverse needs of industries and institutions involved in processing, fabrication, testing, and characterization of composites across various domains. Our focus lies particularly on polymer composites, catering to transportation, civil and mechanical structures, energy, and health sectors. Aligned with Anna University's commitment to technological excellence, our centre aims to collaborate closely with industries to address their requirements in four key areas: Testing and Certification, Training and Education, Consultancy and Research and Development. We invite industries to leverage our expertise and resources and welcome any suggestions to further enhance our activities. By collaborating with us, you contribute to the advancement of the composite materials industry and contribute to the betterment of society at large. We extend our sincere invitation to your esteemed organization to support our endeavors and be a part of our journey towards excellence in composite materials research and innovation. Together, let's shape the future of composite materials technology.

Vision

  • To be a globally leading Centre for composite materials testing and collaborate to facilitate the needs of industries for their product development in the SDG scenario.

Mission

  • To provide a sophisticated testing facility to validate and assure the industrial quality standards for composite materials used in various engineering applications.
  • To provide quality Research, Development and Technology transfer to potential industries.
  • To co-ordinate and collborate with Industrial Stakeholders.
  • To foster effective knowledge transfer between students, researchers and industrial partners.
